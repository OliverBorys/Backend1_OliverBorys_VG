Here you can read about how returns and refunds work.

How it works?

  • You may return items within 30 days of delivery.
  • The product must be unused and in its original condition.
  • A refund is issued once the return has been received and approved.

Guidelines

  • Save your receipt/order confirmation.
  • Pack the item well to avoid damage.
  • Refunds are issued to the same account the purchase was made from.

FAQ

  • Refunds usually appear within 3–5 business days.
  • Shipping costs are reimbursed according to our terms.
  • Sale items may be subject to special return conditions.
